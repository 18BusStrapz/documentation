# Pieces for Developers Support Hub

Here is where you can find quick solutions for your questions or needs, or reach out to the Pieces team.

<Info>
  Before creating a ticket, please use the navigation links to the left to take a look at our product specific Help, Tutorials, Power Tips, and How-To Videos to try and address your issue or question.
</Info>

If you still cannot find an answer to your question or solution to your issue, please use one of the following options to get in touch with the Pieces team.

## GitHub Issues

Our goal is to create a community-driven support experience. We invite you to check out our Github Issues to search for similar issues or submit your own.

We are very active in that community and you should get a response quickly.

[Go to issues](https://github.com/pieces-app/support/issues)

If you prefer not to use GitHub, please [submit a support ticket](https://getpieces.typeform.com/to/mCjBSIjF#page=docs-sidebar). We will respond to you as soon as we can!
